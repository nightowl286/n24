§ What is N24?

N24 is a shortened form of the 'N24SWD' acronym, which stands for the 'Non-24-hour sleep-wake disorder'. It is a circadian rhythm disorder in which an individual's day length (awake + asleep time combined) does not add up to 24 hours.
